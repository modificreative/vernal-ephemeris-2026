♈ ARIES SEASON OVERVIEW

Dates: March 20 - April 19

Element: Cardinal Fire

Ruling Planet: Mars

Tarot Card: The Emperor (IV)

Theme: Initiation, Courage, Action

Aries season ignites spring with bold, pioneering energy. This is the time of new beginnings, courageous action, and direct self-expression. Under Mars's warrior influence, we learn the value of independence, assertiveness, and taking initiative.

📁ingresses/
☉mar_20_spring_equinox.dat

Spring Equinox - Sun Enters Aries - March 20, 2026 (14:46 UTC)

The astrological new year begins as the Sun enters Aries at 0°. Day and night balance in perfect equilibrium before light begins its ascent. This is the moment of fresh starts, new initiatives, and the awakening of warrior spirit.

Themes: Bold beginnings, courage to lead, direct self-assertion, pioneering new territory.

Tarot: 2 of Wands - Bold vision and personal power held in balance; the world at your fingertips.

Tarot: 6 of Pentacles - Generous exchange and balanced reciprocity in love and resources.
